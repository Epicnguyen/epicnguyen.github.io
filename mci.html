01: Master Crafters Incorporated Logo

2 / 4

The Brief:

Stuck for design ideas, I asked a friend of mine if we could run through a design brief for a fictional company that he used in a TTRPG. The company is called Master Crafters Incorporated, a galactic super conglomorate that has dealing in "...all forms of industry. It's kinda like space amazon." My friend wanted a color pallette that used gold, gray and a dark purple, but also wanted the logo to have a steampunk feeling. After attempting to adhere to these requirements, the class agreed that I did a good job with the brief that was given, but I should try it again with the traditional Steampunk colors as well. The result is the logo depicted above.
