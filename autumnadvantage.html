A Simple Project:

So for one of my graphic design projects, the class was given a simple assignment: We were to design a fashion catalogue representing fall fasion, using Indesign. WE were suggested to use an existing brand and I think the idea was that we'd use either royalt free photos of people or images of fashion models. However, I made the mistake of bringing this porject up with my friend group outside of class. One of them said "D&D themed fashion" and my brain didn't rest until this idea was created.

I decided to design one fal outfit for each D&D subclass, That can't be too hard right? How many are there? Oh you know...over 103 official subclasses...But I did it, as you will see in the next slide. The final result Is simply rthe ones I found the best.
