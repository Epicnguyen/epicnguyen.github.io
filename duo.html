The "Company":

I was tasked (by myself) with making a brandbook for a company that "doesn't exist." This company would be called the Department of Unearthly Occurences, or DUO for short. This company would probably, maybe, function similar to the Men In Black, where they clean up and generally obsure any supernatural occurences from the eyes of the general public. As such I felt I needed to effectively design two logos for the brand book: their "official" logo and their "actual" logo. So I would have ended up making a dummy company for them...something like Compass Security Solusions, or CSS or something...
