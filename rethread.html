The Assignment:

In my class on Adobe XD, we were tasked with designing an app. As someone who had seen a strange increase in Augmented Reality Games, I started to wonder if that technology could be put to use for something other than entertainment. So I thought up the Rethread app. I wanted the app to take a photo of you or look at previous photos and allow you to design clothing, hairstyles and accessories upon yourself. The app would recognaze how the augmented additions would shape to your body, similar to how Snapchat's filters contort to the angle of your face. From there it would be as simple as picking colors and patterns. I just need an app like this to exist, so I went forward with the project in hopes that someone with the ability to make it sees the idea and becomes inspired.
